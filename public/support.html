About the App

SquadIQ is a youth soccer coaching app built by a coach, for coaches. It helps you manage your roster, plan lineups, run better practices, and track standings — all in one place.

Access the app at squadiq-coach.vercel.app

Frequently Asked Questions

Does SquadIQ work offline?

Yes. SquadIQ caches your roster, lineups, practice plans, and drill library locally, so you can view and edit them without an internet connection — perfect for the sideline when reception is spotty. Any changes you make while offline are queued automatically and sync as soon as you're back online. A small "offline" indicator in the corner lets you know when you're working from cached data.

Can I manage more than one team?

Yes — Premium Coach supports up to 4 teams, with full data separation between them and a quick team switcher.

How does the AI Lineup Planner work?

It uses your players' position ratings and the "everybody plays" rule to automatically generate balanced lineups across all four quarters, including goalkeeper rotation.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is stored securely with Supabase, encrypted in transit via HTTPS, and never shared with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.

I'm having trouble logging in. What should I do?

Try resetting your password from the login screen. If you still need help, email support@gavamotion.com and we'll get you back in.
