Features

Everything a coach needs.
Nothing they don't.

Five connected tools that replace spreadsheets, group chats, and notebooks — built specifically for the way youth soccer actually works.

01 — MY TEAM

Your roster, finally organized.

All your players, positions, and ratings in one place.

Build a complete profile for every player — jersey number, preferred positions, and per-position skill ratings. Rate strikers, defenders, midfielders, and goalkeepers independently so the rest of the app knows where each kid shines. Run multiple teams from one account with instant switching.

  • Add, edit, delete players
  • Per-position star ratings
  • Multi-team support (up to 4)
  • One-tap team switcher
Every Function in My Team
Player Profiles
Name, jersey number, photo, and notes for each player on your roster.
Position Ratings
Rate each player 1-5 stars across multiple positions — GK, DEF, MID, FWD.
Preferred Position
Mark a primary position so the AI knows where your player belongs.
Team Creation
Set team name, age division, colors, and roster size when creating a new team.
Quick Team Switcher
Bottom-sheet modal on mobile to jump between up to 4 teams instantly.
Safe Deletion
Confirmation modal before deleting a team or player — no accidents.
02 — LINEUP

AI does your sub plans.

No more pre-game spreadsheets at 6am.

Pick a formation, mark your available players, and tap one button — the AI Lineup Planner generates a balanced four-quarter lineup using player ratings, position preferences, and the "everybody plays" rule. Goalkeeper rotation handled automatically. Override anything with drag-and-drop.

  • One-tap AI Lineup Planner
  • Automatic GK rotation
  • Equal playing time enforcement
  • Drag-and-drop overrides
Every Function in Lineup
AI Lineup Planner
Generates balanced quarters in seconds based on ratings and availability.
Formation Selector
Pick your formation — 4-3-3, 3-2-2, 2-3-2, and more based on team size.
Quarter-by-Quarter View
See each of the four quarters separately and switch with a tap.
Availability Toggle
Mark players in/out before generating — sick kids and no-shows are easy.
GK Rotation
Automatically rotates goalkeepers across quarters so no one's stuck in goal.
Manual Drag & Drop
Override any AI suggestion by dragging players to different positions.
Playing Time Counter
Live count of quarters per player so you can confirm fairness at a glance.
Save & Reuse
Lineups save automatically — pick up where you left off next game day.
03 — SKETCH BOARD

Draw plays kids understand.

The tactical board that finally works on mobile.

A fully interactive field with players you can drag, arrows you can draw, and movements you can map. Switch tools mid-play, erase mistakes, undo and redo anything. Save multiple boards with custom names — keep your best plays, formations, and set pieces ready for halftime.

  • Drag players, draw arrows
  • Erase, undo, redo
  • Multiple renameable boards
  • Mobile and tablet optimized
Every Function in Sketch
Player Pieces
Drag offensive and defensive markers anywhere on the field.
Formation Presets
Drop a full formation onto the field in one tap — then adjust.
Arrow Tool
Draw movement lines for runs, passes, and player rotations.
Ball Marker
Add a ball anywhere on the field to show possession and target zones.
Eraser
Wipe individual arrows or markers without clearing the whole board.
Undo & Redo
Step backward and forward through every change you make.
Multiple Boards
Keep separate tabs for different plays, drills, or scenarios.
Rename Boards
Double-click or long-press a tab to give it a custom name.
Clear Field
Wipe an entire board fast when you want to start a new sketch.
04 — PRACTICE

69 drills. Smart plans.

Build a structured practice in minutes — or let AI do it.

A curated library of 69 drills covering warm-ups, technique, possession, finishing, defending, and small-sided games. Build practices manually by picking drills and setting durations, or tap AI Practice Plan and get a balanced session generated around the skills you want to focus on.

  • 69 age-appropriate drills
  • AI Practice Plan generator
  • Build & save custom plans
  • Step-by-step instructions
Every Function in Practice
AI Practice Plan
Pick a focus area and duration — AI builds a balanced session for you.
Drill Library
69 drills organized by skill, age group, and intensity level.
Category Filters
Warm-up, dribbling, passing, shooting, defending, small-sided games.
Drill Details
Diagrams, setup instructions, coaching points, and progressions.
Custom Plans
Mix any drills into your own practice plan, in any order.
Set Durations
Set how long each drill runs — total time updates automatically.
Saved Plans
Save plans to reuse week after week or copy and modify them.
Field-Ready View
Clean session view to follow drill-by-drill during practice.
05 — STANDINGS

Always know where you stand.

Real-time standings, auto-refreshed.

Pulls live standings directly from MatchTrak and TeamSideline so you're never refreshing a clunky league website at halftime. Track multiple divisions in tabs, see your team highlighted, and let the data refresh itself in the background.

  • MatchTrak & TeamSideline support
  • Auto-refresh every 6 hours
  • Multiple division tabs
  • Your team highlighted
Every Function in Standings
MatchTrak Scraper
Pulls live standings from MatchTrak — all 12 teams of your division.
TeamSideline Scraper
Same live standings pulled from TeamSideline league pages.
Multiple Divisions
Add tabs for each division you follow — sibling teams, rival groups.
Team Highlight
Your team is highlighted in the table so you spot it at a glance.
Auto-Refresh
Data refreshes automatically if it's older than six hours.
Manual Refresh
Tap to force an immediate refresh right after a game ends.
Full Stats
Wins, losses, ties, points, goals for, goals against, and goal differential.
Renameable Tabs
Give each standings tab a custom name to stay organized.
