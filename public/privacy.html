Privacy Policy

We built SquadIQ for coaches — not advertisers. Here's exactly how we handle your data.

Last updated: May 5, 2025

Overview

SquadIQ - Soccer Coach ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by Gava Motion. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user. We take your privacy seriously and will never sell your data.

Data We Collect

Account Information Your name and email address when you create an account.
Team & Roster Data Team names, player names, positions, and ratings you enter into the app.
Lineup & Practice Plans Lineups, substitution plans, and practice sessions you create.
Usage Data Basic app interaction data to improve performance and fix bugs.
Payment Information Processed securely by Stripe. We never store your card details.

How We Use Your Data

To provide and improve the SquadIQ app and its features.
To sync your data across devices via Supabase.
To process subscription payments via Stripe.
To send account-related emails (receipts, password resets).
To diagnose crashes and fix technical issues.

Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

Supabase Secure cloud database for storing your account and team data. Privacy Policy →
Stripe Payment processing. We never see or store your card details. Privacy Policy →

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Your data is only shared with the third-party services listed above, solely to operate the app.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

SquadIQ is designed for coaches, not players. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Player names entered by coaches are treated as coaching data, not personal accounts.
